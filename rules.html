1 GENERAL
  • 1.1 No hitting ("friendly" shoving is acceptable)
  • 1.2 Newbies are off limits (except to other newbies)
  • 1.3 The two newbie lines will match up against each other, please be considerate and change when the newbies come on
  • 1.4 Slapshots are only permitted when clear

    • 2 GAMEPLAY
  • 2.1 All penalties are enforced by the opposing team (within reason), the offending team will return the puck to the opposing team
  • 2.2 Offsides will be enforced by the opposing team, the opposing team will start from their zone
  • 2.3 Icing will be enforced by the opposing team, the opposing team will start from their zone

    • 3 SUB WAIVERS
  • 3.1 If a player cannont attend a game, a sub will be found to replace that player
  • 3.2 If both teams have players that cannot attend, then the prior game's losing team will have first pick from the pool of subs
  • 3.3 If the prior game resulted in a tie, the team requiring more subs will get to pick first from the pool of subs
